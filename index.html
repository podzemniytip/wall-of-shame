How It Works

1. Pay a fixed fee to post a short message (≤32 chars) on-chain.

2. Your message overwrites the previous one — they lose their fee, no refund.

3. 93% of every fee goes to the reward pool, 7% to the dev.

4. If your message stands unchallenged for the full delay — claim the entire pool.

5. The message stays forever. A new post starts the game again.
